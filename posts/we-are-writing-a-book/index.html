It’s official. We’re writing a book!

Mastering Identus: A Developer Handbook, will serve as practical guide on installing, building and maintaining real-world Identus applications.

Who are we?

Roberto Carvajal and myself have worked together on web and mobile projects since 2006 as Covelop, a software development agency, helping primarily music industry artists, labels, and DJs sell their music online.

In the past few years, we’ve grown interested in how digital identity will play a role in society and how we can help during this important, inevitable transition.

We’ve teamed up with some like-minded individuals and am now building SSI solutions as Good Future

Why write a book?

While learning Smart Contract development on the Cardano blockchain, we had an opportunity (Thank You Tony Rose!) to learn about version 2 of Atala PRISM, a Self-Soveign Identity platform, and we were inspired to focus our sights on building SSI products and tooling.

In 2023 we built Csign, a way to privately sign, certity, and verify agreements between parties, using SSI and blockchain. Csign was built on Atala PRISM, which was open sourced and donated to the Hyperledger Foundation, and has now been renamed to Identus.

We learned so much by building a real-world project and wanted to share our tricks and tips with other Identus builders, so they could get set up and building quicker than we did.

This book was funded by a grant from Project Catalyst, Fund 11. Voted on by Cardano wallet holders, we are honored to be able to capture our experience and make it available to the community. To all those who voted, we humbly thank you for your support!
